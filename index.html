About Me

I am a Postdoctoral researcher with a passion for understanding the Quark-Gluon Plasma, heavy-ion collisions, and neutrino oscillations. My work with the NA61/SHINE collaboration at CERN in Geneva has led to numerous presentations at international conferences, advancing our understanding of fundamental physics.

As an Assistant Professor at the University of Warsaw, I am currently focused on neutrino physics, working with the T2K and Super-Kamiokande collaborations in Japan to explore the elusive properties of neutrinos.
